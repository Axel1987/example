About us

Talent Savant is a corporation on a simple yet revolutionary mission: to treat every party in our business with respect. To offer full transparency and deliver the kind truth, act with integrity, form longlasting and meaningful partnerships, and operate with efficiency and speed.

We are radically ProClient. Talent Savant is only the most affordable talent agency, we pay our Talent Partners more per role than any other big firm, so we attract and retain the best talent across any sector, in every geography in the country.

We are radically ProEmployee. We pledge to invest the vast majority of our profits back into our employees, because it is the right thing to do. We are a familyowned business and do not hoard cash or answer to shareholders.

We are radically ProCandidate. We do not engage in deceptive agency practices or sell human beings as commodities. Instead, we offer jobseekers free, practical, and easytofollow advice that is normally only reserved for elite agency candidates. When one of our Talent Partners reaches out to you, she or he has a job that is actively interviewing, in your geography that matches your skill set. Period.

We are radically ProFamily. 100% remote model allows us to provide our employees with flexible schedules, honoring sacred worklife balance.These values are the beating heart of our business. We are proud to call ourselves ProHuman.

Executive team

Ingrid Johnson, Founder and CEO

Ingrid Johnson, a single mom and former agency recruiter. Ingrid's dream was to create a company that both offered a radical improvement in worklife balance and funneled the vast majority of profits back into the hands of the employees. This mission, along with her experience in the recruiting agency world, guided her to create a revolutionary business model within the Talent Acquisition market. Ingrid identified several areas in need of improvement including client fee percentages, recruiter commission structure, and job candidate attraction and retention. She also found great need for transparency and integrity throughout the recruiting, hiring and retention cycles. Talent Savant was born. This ProHuman, MissionDriven organization operates based on Ingrid's four guiding principles of Transparency, Integrity, Partnership and Speed.

Ingrid loves writing, doing the right thing, and has a shameless affection for boxed wine. She lives in Seattle, WA, with her son, Rex.
